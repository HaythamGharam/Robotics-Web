ABOUT US

What is Lydia Jobs?

Lydia Jobs is a global leader in connecting people and jobs. Every day, Lydia Jobs makes every workplace happier and more productive by transforming the way employers and candidates find the right fit. are here to simplify the job search and the entire application process. Lydia Jobs also allows candidates to easily search for the opportunities they want, thanks to search engines with many filters (location, salary, type of contract, job title, etc.).

What do we aim for?

Lydia Jobs's mission is to create a connection between the recruiter who posts a job offer and the candidate who is looking for a job. Lydia Jobs will allow candidates to upload their CV on a resume database to potentially be contacted by recruiters. Candidates can also apply directly to advertisements offered by companies.

How Lydia Jobs works?

Like the famous "Tinder", Lydia Jobs' goal is to "match" candidates with recruiters. The candidate provides information on his research, skills, experiences, location, etc. For his part, the recruiter informs his needs, the skills they are looking for. Using an algorithm that analyzes this data, candidates see a list of job openings that best match their skills
